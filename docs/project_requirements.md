# Project Requirements

## Business Request

The organization wants to analyze workforce and project spending to identify departments and projects that may be exceeding their allocated budgets.

Departments are given budgets that are allocated for a **2-year period**, and management wants to determine whether the **current yearly expenses can be covered within this budget**.

## Key Questions

- Which departments are at risk of exceeding their budget?
- Which projects contribute most to department spending?
- Are salary and project costs exceeding department allocations?
- Can the yearly expenses be supported by the 2-year department budget?

## Data Sources

The analysis uses multiple datasets including:

- Employee information
- Salary data
- Department budgets
- Project details and project budgets

## Project Objective

The goal of this project is to develop a **Power BI dashboard** that helps management monitor:

- Workforce spending
- Project budget allocation
- Department financial performance
- Potential budget risks

The dashboard allows stakeholders to quickly identify departments or projects that require financial attention.
